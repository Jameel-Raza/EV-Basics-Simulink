# Battery and Battery Management Systems (BMS)

This section contains MATLAB Simulink models related to
EV battery fundamentals and battery management systems.

## Topics Covered
- Lead Acid and Li-Ion battery modeling
- State of Charge (SOC) and State of Health (SOH) estimation
- Battery pack modeling
- Passive cell balancing
- Active cell balancing (BMS)

## Intended Use
These models are designed for:
- EV laboratory experiments
- Conceptual understanding of battery behavior
- Academic learning and exploration

Some models may be preliminary and will be refined over time.
